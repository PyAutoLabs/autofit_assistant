# Your Domain Literature Wiki — Index

Top-level navigation. See `AGENTS.md` for the schema and how the assistant should use
this wiki.

**This wiki is intentionally empty.** It is yours: as you ingest the papers of your
field (via `af_ingest_paper`, or by asking the assistant to "become my inference
assistant for <your domain>"), concept, entity and source pages appear here and this
index grows into your domain's map.

## Getting started

Ask the assistant:

```
I work on <your field>. Here are the key papers for the analyses I run:
<arXiv IDs or PDFs>. Ingest them into your literature wiki.
```
